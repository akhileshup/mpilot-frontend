FAQ
Common questions
Do I need a WhatsApp Business API account?+
MPilot uses ChatMitra to provide a virtual WhatsApp number. You don't need your own WABA. Just connect your ChatMitra API key in Settings and you're live.
Can I try before I pay?+
Yes — the Free plan is permanent, no credit card required. It includes 1 QR code, up to 100 leads, and Google Business connection. Upgrade when you're ready to scale.
How does the QR → WhatsApp capture work?+
Your MPilot QR links to a WhatsApp pre-filled message. When a customer scans and sends the message, they're automatically added to your CRM with their name and number. No forms, no apps.
Can I manage multiple branches?+
Yes, on the Pro plan. The first branch is included; additional branches are ₹499/month each. Each branch has its own QR codes, CRM, analytics, and WhatsApp automation.
Is my customer data safe?+
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share customer data. You can export or delete your data at any time.
