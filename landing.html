FAQ
Common questions
Do I need a WhatsApp Business account?+
No — MPilot connects you to WhatsApp through our managed service. You don't need to apply for any accounts or do any technical setup whatsoever.
Can I start for free?+
Yes — the Free plan is permanent with no credit card required. It includes 1 QR code, up to 100 contacts, and Google Business connection. Upgrade whenever you're ready.
How does the QR contact capture work?+
Your MPilot QR code opens a pre-filled WhatsApp message when scanned. When the customer sends it, they're automatically added to your contact list. No forms, no apps needed.
Can I manage multiple branches?+
Yes — the Pro plan supports multiple locations from one account. Each branch has its own QR codes, customer list, and campaigns. Additional branches are available at a low monthly fee.
Is my customer data safe?+
All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell or share your customer data. You have full ownership and can export or delete it at any time.
What support is available?+
All plans include email support. Growth and Pro plans get priority support. Agency plan includes a dedicated account manager for onboarding and ongoing help.
